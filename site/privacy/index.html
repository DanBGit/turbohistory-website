Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026. Short version: we run Google Analytics to see which books people are interested in, and if you give us your email we use it to tell you when books are free. We do not sell anything to anyone, ever.

Who we are

This site is run by Turbo History, an independent publisher of short history books. Contact us about anything on this page at privacy@turbohistory.com.

What we collect

Analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 to count visits and see which book pages are popular. It sets cookies and collects things like your approximate location, device and which pages you viewed. Your IP address is anonymised. In the UK, EEA, Switzerland, Brazil and Canada none of this runs unless you press Accept. Everywhere else it runs by default and you can turn it off any time using "Your privacy choices" in the footer.

Your email, if you give it. If you sign up for free book alerts we store your email address and, if you told us, which book you were interested in. We use it for one thing: telling you when Turbo History books are free or newly released. We do not sell, rent or share it. Every email has a one-click unsubscribe.

Server logs. Our host keeps standard web server logs (IP, page, time) for security and troubleshooting.

Legal basis

Analytics: your consent, where consent is required. Email alerts: your consent, given when you subscribe. Server logs: our legitimate interest in keeping the site up and secure.

Who else sees it

Google (analytics), and our email delivery provider when you subscribe. Our web host stores the site and logs. Nobody else. We never sell data.

How long we keep it

Analytics data: 14 months. Your email: until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it.

Your rights

You can ask us for a copy of what we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email privacy@turbohistory.com and we will sort it. If you are in the UK or EEA and think we have handled your data badly, you can also complain to your national data protection authority.

Cookies

Only Google Analytics cookies, and only with consent where consent is required. No advertising cookies, no tracking pixels, no third-party ad networks. You can change your choice any time via "Your privacy choices" in the footer.

Buying the books

Our books are sold by Amazon, not by this website. We never see your payment details. Amazon's own privacy policy covers anything you do on their site.

Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date at the top.
